Compost

Royal Crest Farm Compost is made from goat manure, cow manure, hay, sawdust, and wood chips from our farm in Spencer, MA. Our product is environmentally friendly, free of harmful chemicals or residues and will help provide beautiful flower gardens, abundant vegetables and healthy lawns. The composting process begins when we combine hay and shavings with cow and goat manure in long windrows and ‘cook’ it for at least three months at a temperature of 130° – 150. This process filters out most weed seeds which can't survive this process. When complete, the compost is screened at 1/2’ particles. We recommend using the Royal Crest Farm compost to amend all types of soil. Plantings will establish better root systems, be more tolerant to drought and get the nutrients they need for healthy growth and disease resistance.
